Pancake

Description

Everyone loves the idea of waking up to a breakfast of warm pancakes topped with delights like fresh berries and sweet maple syrup, but cooking an entire pancake breakfast can be time-consuming and messy. Luckily, TikTok has a solution for that: Enter the lazy girl pancake bowl, a social media trend that content creators are making their go-to breakfastâ€¦for good reason.

There are hundreds of videos about the lazy girl pancake bowl on TikTok alone, with users sharing their own take on the breakfast fad using hashtags like #PancakeBowl and #LazyGirlPancakeBowl. Always on the hunt for an easy breakfast idea, I was intrigued by the berry-topped creations, so I tried making the lazy girl pancake bowl in my own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Pancake mix
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Half a banana
  • Topping of your liking

Steps

  1. Combine everything but the toppings in a rectangular glass dish
  2. Put the glass dish in a microwave
  3. Bake in a microwave for a couple of minutes
  4. take it out and add your toppings
